Who I am?

Phsycologist, researcher, storyteller, and Texan who's spent the past two decades studying courage, vulnerability, shame, and empathy. Professor at the University of Houston, where she hold the Huffington Foundation Endowed Chair. She is also a visiting professor in management at the University of Texas at Austin McCombs School of Business.
